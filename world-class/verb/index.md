# 动词短语
* get ... back: 拿回。get my umbreall back.
* come out of : 从某个地方出来。come out of the park.
* go into: 进入...。go into the park.
* give/buy ... to : give/buy it to me == give me it
* in the front of ... : 在什么前边
* keep off:不接近、避开
* look out:当心
* help yourself:自便。
